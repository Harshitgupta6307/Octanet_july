A gym, short for gymnasium, is a dedicated facility designed for physical exercise, strength training, and fitness activities. It provides a space equipped with various exercise machines, weights, cardio equipment, and often offers group fitness classes or personal training services. Gyms are popular among individuals seeking to improve their overall health, build strength, increase endurance, lose weight, or simply maintain an active lifestyle.
